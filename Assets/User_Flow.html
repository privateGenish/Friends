User Onboarding
User downloads and opens the Friends app
Prompt to sign up or log in
Sign up
Enter phone number
Log in
Enter login credentials
OTP is sent to the phone for verification
Enter OTP
Create profile by uploading face picture, two additional photos, and filling in bio
Profile is created
User is taken to the home screen to start exploring the app
Group Formation
User selects the option to create a new group
Fill in group details: name, description, category
Invite friends to join the group
Waiting for friends to accept invitations
At least one friend accepts the invitation
Group becomes active
Group members can edit the group profile
Start looking for other groups to match with
Matching Process
Browse or search for other groups based on interests or categories
Swipe right to indicate interest or left to pass
Did at least half of the group members swipe right?
Yes
Other group receives a notification of interest
No
Back to browsing
Did at least half of the other group members swipe right?
Yes
Match is made
No
Back to browsing
Matched groups can start chatting or planning activities together
Messaging Flow
Two chat options available: Mutual Group Chat or Individual Chat
Mutual Group Chat: Shared group chat for discussions and activities
Individual Chat: Private one-on-one conversations
Text Messaging: Send text messages in group chats
Media Messaging: Share photos, videos, and other media
Internal Group Discussions: Private communication within the group
Commenting on Matches: Discuss potential matches or interactions
General Messaging: Plan activities and share thoughts internally
